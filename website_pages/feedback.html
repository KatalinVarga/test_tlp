We need your feedback!

Through out the course of your studies, we will send you feedback surverys for you to complete. These surveys allow you to provide us with honest and anonymous feedback regarding all aspects of your studies, from the support you receive when applying to study, to the quality of the teaching materials and the functionality of the TLP.

 

The feedback is incredibly important to us - your comments and suggestions really do help to shape the future of MLA College. Please take the opportunity to complete the surveys when you receive them, it really does make a difference.

 

How we use your feedback

This video explains the importance of your feedback, and gives examples of how what you tell us helps us to ensure the best possible student experience.
