The videos and documents linked below have been provided for you to use as reference materials during the course of your studies.

They have been carefully selected from the entire MLA College catelogue - for this reason, they may include references to such things as formative assessment; specific details regarding summative assessment and other lectures from the MLA College module that they were originally produced for. We have provided all transcripts for these lectures, however, due to the fact that these lectures are not assessed we have excluded any formative assessment.

 

Whilst you are not directly assessed on these materials it is recommended that you take the time work through them, the topics covered will help with the work you will undertake to complete your studies and assessment.
