Student experience services

Studying via distance learning can be difficult. Often, balancing work and life is tough enough without having to factor in time to watch lectures and complete your assessment. Life can often throw up unexpected events which can add to day-to-day stresses. We understand that part-time distance learning can be tough, and we are here to help you.

Don't suffer in silence

In order for us to help you, we need you to let us know if you are struggling. We are here to support you through your studies and you should be confident that you can contact us whenever you need to.

The MLA College Student Experience team is available to support you, so please contact them should you need them.

Student Representative

At MLA College, we recognise the essential role students play in shaping our community through their feedback. Our student representative acts as a crucial connection between students and MLA Committees, serving as a point of contact for each student.

 

The student representative will work alongside Abi and Charlie to improve student voice, ensuring that concerns, suggestions, and needs are heard and addressed. By collecting and relaying this information, the student rep helps bridge the gap between the student body and the college administration, facilitating a collaborative approach to enhance the overall student experience.

 

You can contact your Student Representative via a dedicated email address:student.rep@mla.ac.uk

 

We are continuously encouraging our students to express interest in becoming a Student Rep. If you are a current student and would like further details on the role, please contact our Student Experience Team.
