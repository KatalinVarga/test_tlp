Optimum audio
Due to the varying nature and performance of laptop and PC speakers we recommend that you use headphones when listening to the lecture materials and videos housed within the TLP.
Web browser

An HTML5 compatible web browser is required for the embedded videos to function. The latest versions of commonly used web browsers (Google Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge and Opera), have this capability whilst older versions may have problems (for example, IE 8 cannot open the embedded videos). We recommend that you update your preferred browser to the latest build.

 

Video playback speed

Should you wish, you can add an extension to your browser to allow you adjust the playback speed of your lecture videos. An excellent example for this for Chrome is the Video Speed Controller which is free and available via the Chrome Web Store. Once installed, you should open your Chrome extensions manager; locate the Video Speed Controller extension; click on 'Details'; and then ensure that you turn on 'Allow access to file URLs' (ensure the slider is to the right and the icon is blue) - this will allow the Video Speed Controller extension to work with your TLP.

 

Please note: MLA College cannot accept any responsibility for third party content, and we are unable to provide any technical support for the Video Speed Controller extension, or any other extensions you install.

PDF readers
If required, Adobe Acrobat can be installed to allow you open pdf files found on the TLP. Alternative pdf readers can be found with a quick internet search.
