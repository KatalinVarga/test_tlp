IMPORTANT INFORMATION REGARDING YOUR ASSESSMENT HAND IN DATES

You can find exact assessment hand in dates for this module via Turnitin. If you are in any doubt as to these dates, ensure that you check Turnitin - it is extremely important that you meet all assessment deadlines.

 

Dr Paul Wright has produced this useful guide to using Turnitin. As you will see, this video is part of series of tutorials Dr Wright created that focuss on academic misconduct, and we would recommend that you watch these videos as well.

Please ensure that you read these instructions for submitting your coursework.
The course team recommends you ensure that you have completed the lectures for each individual section before you attempt the summative questions. You should also ensure that you have completed the formative questions to your satisfaction, as a full understanding of these should help you to complete the summative questions.

Please make sure you have a careful look at the learning outcomes for the module, the lectures and at the mark scheme before you start your assessment. You MUST complete and return your assessment cover sheet with your coursework. If you have any concerns or questions regarding this, please contact your tutor.

To help the course team when your work is being marked, please ensure that you number your answers carefully in your coursework document, and lay out the document clearly and neatly. Don’t forget to spell check your work, and proof read it carefully before submission.
