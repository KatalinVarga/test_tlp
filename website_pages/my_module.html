Teaching and learning materials
The lectures below have been provided for you to use as reference materials during the course of your studies. The lectures have been carefully selected from the entire MLA College catelogue - for this reason, the lectures may include references to such things as formative assessment; level of study and other lectures from the MLA College module that they were originally produced for. Please do not be confused by any such reference, the lectures provided are the lectures you should study.

If you have any worries or questions regarding any of the lectures' content, please contact your tutor.
